# Content rewrite rules

The legacy question generator is **not production content**. It is retained only as a temporary development fixture and must not be used to claim a 270-question study bank.

## Required review for every published question

1. Confirm its fact or decision against Utah Driver Handbook REV 3.2026.
2. Give the item one unique learning objective and a stable handbook topic reference.
3. Write a clear stem, four plausible choices, one unambiguous correct answer, and an explanatory rationale.
4. Check that it is not a rewording of another item or an answer-pattern duplicate.
5. Test it with the intended visual, when a sign, traffic conflict, or road geometry is central to the rule.

## Release gate

Do not call the bank complete until it has 300 independently authored and reviewed records distributed according to `data/content-blueprint.json`, with every topic represented and all test questions checked by a second reviewer.
